GeograPhyl

This site uses a heuristic applied to the global public SARS-CoV-2 phylogenetic tree to identify groups of sequences from an area that seem to share a recent common ancestor from outside the region. These likely reflect cases of an infected traveler entering a region with a new strain, followed by local spread. Please post an issue at the github or email me at jmcbroom@ucsc.edu if you have questions or feedback.
